fireplace_lib::handlers

Module fireplace_lib::handlers::keyboard [−] [src]

Collection of common Keyboard related types.

The KeyboardHandler and KeyHandler may be used for global key handling, while the KeyPattern just describes a common structure of a key action, that may be handled by the compositor. The KeyPattern is therefor often used in other handlers reacting to key presses.
